CIVIL ENGINEERING

Civil engineering is a branch of engineering that focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of the physical structures and systems that people use in everyday life. This includes things like buildings, roads, bridges, water supply systems, and transportation networks. It is considered one of the oldest engineering disciplines because it deals with basic human needs such as shelter, mobility, and access to clean water. Civil engineers apply scientific and mathematical principles to plan and create safe, efficient, and durable infrastructure.
In simple terms, civil engineering is the field responsible for building and improving the environment we live in, making communities more functional, organized, and sustainable.
